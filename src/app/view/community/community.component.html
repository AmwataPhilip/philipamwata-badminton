My commitment to growing badminton and creating positive impact through the sport in Kenya, Luxembourg, and beyond.

My Vision

Badminton has transformed my life, teaching me discipline, strategic thinking, and the value of community. I believe in the power of this sport to create positive change across communities and borders.

My mission is to make badminton more accessible, particularly in underserved communities in Kenya, while also contributing to the growth and development of the sport in Luxembourg. Through grassroots programs, coaching initiatives, and technology integration, I aim to build bridges between diverse badminton communities and create pathways for the next generation of players.
