Professional Badminton Player from Kenya

I began my badminton journey in 2009 and quickly developed a passion that has shaped my life ever since. As a Kenyan athlete who has competed internationally, I bring unique perspectives and experiences to the court.

My playing style focuses on singles competition, where I leverage my strengths in speed, jumping ability, and powerful smashes. I've trained under Kenya's top-ranked singles player for over 3 years, developing both technical precision and strategic thinking.

Beyond my playing career, I'm deeply committed to growing the sport of badminton in both Kenya and Luxembourg. I believe that badminton isn't just a sport but a vehicle for developing discipline, strategic thinking, and cultural exchange.
